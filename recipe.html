Fresh Garden Salad

A healthy and refreshing salad packed with fresh vegetables and a light dressing.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 20 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
  2. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and carrot in a bowl.
  3. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy.

Nutrition

Calories 120 kcal
Protein 3 g
Carbohydrates 10 g
Fat 8 g
